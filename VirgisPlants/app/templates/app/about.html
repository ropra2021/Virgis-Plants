History of Virgis Plants

Starting late 2019, Virginia started buying and growing rare plants she purchased online. After the plants were grown she would sell them through different online channels, such as Etsy and Facebook Marketplace. In the summer of 2020 Virginia started travelling to plant nurseries in Texas, California, and Mexico, where she purchased rare houseplants. As a result she had an extensive inventory of over 200 houseplants. Throughout the rest of 2020 she continued to acquire, propogate and sell houseplants. As of 2021, she has continued to grow virgisplants, with plans of gaining an international presence in the plant community. In the first year of operations Virginia has grown a following within the state of Texas due to the high quality plants that she provides her clients. The future is bright for vigisplants due to the heart Virginia puts into growing her plants. Due to her understanding of the biology behind each of the plant species she handles, she is able to nurture plants the right way. This results into a beautiful plant that will add a natural beauty to your home for years to come.

As part of Virginias mission to provide her costumers with the rarest plants in the world she continously imports plants from Indonesia, Thailand, New Zeland, Europe, and Brazil.
